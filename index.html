Instructions for Schools / Institutions

Schools and institutions intending to visit the Naval Aviation Museum are requested to notify the Headquarters, Goa Naval Area at least 7 days in advance.

Postal Address:
The Flag Officer Commanding
(for SO (Admin))
Headquarters, Goa Naval Area
Vasco-da-Gama, Goa – 403802

A copy of the request must also be emailed to contact@navalaviationmuseum.in.

One day prior to the visit, please confirm telephonically on 7028730385 and provide group leader details.

After the visit, don't forget to sign and collect the visit completion form from the Museum Office.
